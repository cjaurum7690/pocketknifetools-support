# Privacy Policy for PocketKnife Games

Last updated: March 30, 2026

PocketKnife Games ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. 
This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data — by not collecting it.

## 1. No Data Collection

PocketKnife Games does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. 
Specifically:
- We do not track usage behavior, device information, or user identity.
- We do not use analytics, advertising, or cloud-based user accounts.

## 2. Third-Party Services

There are no external APIs used in PocketKnife Games.
We do not store or send any user-identifiable information.

## 3. Location Data

PocketKnife Games does not request access to your device's 
location and never transmits location data to us or any third party.

## 4. Children's Privacy

PocketKnife Games is not directed at children under 13 and does not 
knowingly collect personal data from anyone.

## 5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in functionality 
or legal requirements. Updates will be posted within the App and/or 
on our support page.
