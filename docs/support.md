## Frequently Asked Questions

**Why does the Weather tool show the wrong location?**
Make sure location permissions are enabled for PocketKnife Tools in your 
device Settings. You can also search for your city manually using the 
search bar.

**Why are my stock prices delayed?**
Stock data is provided by Finnhub and may reflect a slight delay depending 
on market conditions and your data plan.

**Why is the currency rate not updating?**
Currency rates are powered by the Frankfurter API and update in real time. 
Check your internet connection if rates appear stale.

**Why is the Translator not working?**
The Translator requires an active internet connection. It is powered by 
Google Translate and translates as you type.

**My compass seems inaccurate — what should I do?**
Try calibrating your device by moving it in a figure-8 motion a few times. 
Make sure there are no magnetic objects nearby.

**How do I reorder my saved cities or stocks?**
Tap the **Manage Cities** or **Manage Stocks** button, then hold and drag 
the ☰ handle on any row to reorder.

**How do I remove a city or stock?**
Tap **Manage Cities** or **Manage Stocks** and tap the ✕ button on the 
item you want to remove.

**Is PocketKnife Tools free?**
Yes — completely free with no ads and no subscription required.
