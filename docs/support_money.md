## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is PocketKnife Money free?**
Is PocketKnife Money free?
Yes — completely free with no ads and no subscription required.

Is internet access required to use PocketKnife Money?
Almost all features work completely offline. The Stock Tracker requires an internet connection to retrieve live market data from Yahoo Finance (or Finnhub if you've added your own API key). All other tools — calculators, trackers, and reference content — work without any internet connection.

## Privacy

PocketKnife Money does not collect or store any personal data. All your saved data — budgets, goals, subscriptions, and watchlists — is stored locally on your device and never shared.
