# Privacy Policy for PocketKnife Tools

Last updated: March 15, 2026

PocketKnife Tools ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. 
This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data — by not collecting it.

## 1. No Data Collection

PocketKnife Tools does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. 
Specifically:
- We do not track usage behavior, device information, or user identity.
- All preferences, saved cities, watchlists, and settings are stored 
  locally on your device.
- We do not use analytics, advertising, or cloud-based user accounts.

## 2. Third-Party Services

To provide certain features, PocketKnife Tools uses the following 
external APIs:

- **Google Translate API:** Used to translate text between languages. 
  Requests are anonymous and contain only the text and target language.
- **Frankfurter API:** Used to retrieve live currency exchange rates. 
  No personal data is transmitted.
- **Open-Meteo API:** Used to retrieve weather forecasts based on 
  coordinates you provide. No personal data is transmitted.
- **Finnhub API:** Used to retrieve real-time stock quotes and company 
  data. No personal data is transmitted.

These services may log requests for performance or security purposes. 
We do not send any user-identifiable information.

## 3. Location Data

The Compass and Weather tools may request access to your device's 
location. This data is used solely on-device to provide compass headings 
and local weather. It is never transmitted to us or any third party 
beyond the weather API request.

## 4. Children's Privacy

PocketKnife Tools is not directed at children under 13 and does not 
knowingly collect personal data from anyone.

## 5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in functionality 
or legal requirements. Updates will be posted within the App and/or 
on our support page.
